Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Personal Loan?

A Personal Loan is an unsecured loan that provides individuals with funds for personal expenses like weddings, travel, home renovation, medical emergencies, etc. Unlike secured loans, personal loans do not require any collateral or security.

How much personal loan can I get?

The loan amount depends on various factors like your income, credit score, existing debts, and repayment capacity. Typically, banks offer personal loans ranging from ₹50,000 to ₹40 lakhs.

What is the interest rate for a Personal Loan?

Interest rates for personal loans typically range from 9.99% to 24% per annum, depending on the lender, your credit profile, income, and loan tenure. MoneyBridge helps you find the best rates from multiple banks.

How long does it take to get a Personal Loan approved?

With MoneyBridge, you can get instant approval within minutes if you meet the eligibility criteria and have all the required documents. The disbursal can happen within 24-48 hours after approval, depending on the bank's processing time.

Can I get a Personal Loan with a low credit score?

While most banks prefer a credit score of 750 or above, some lenders may consider applications with lower scores but could charge a higher interest rate. MoneyBridge can help you find options that match your credit profile.
